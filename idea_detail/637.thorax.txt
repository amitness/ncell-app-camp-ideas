Idea:
 Apps to alert if you reached the destination while travelling on public transport.
 
Problem Solved:
Reaching new places on public transport is stressfull.
And some people sleep on bus and miss the destination.
 
Functions:
 Gps, Maps, Alarm
 
Targeted audience:
 People visiting new places(tourists), people who travel at night, or people who feel asleep on bus
 
 
Main Benefits:
Reaching new places on public transport is stress free. And you can sleep on bus without worrying you will miss the destination. 
 
Platform: Android
