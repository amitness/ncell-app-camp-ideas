Idea:
 an application to help people in need(Your loved ones, or those registered in our app) during time of emergency and also make yourself save.
 
Problem Solved:
1. Decrease the search area vicinity and those increase chances to save people in any calamity or problem.
2. Easy search for blood of any group in time of emergency.
 
Functions:
 1. Keep track of the user location so that it can be helpful to find/rescue your loved ones(Primary Contacts/ Secondary Contacts) at the time of natural calamities reducing the search vicinity.* Primary contacts: Those contacts whom automatic alert(SmS and push notifcation) would be sent giving them your location, and your safety state(Safe/Unsafe)
*Secondary Contact: Those contacts whom only your safety state would be sent. 
2.  Organize volunteering service in times needed and one can also join the volunteering program if the program is in certain radius of his current location.
3. One could search for blood and send push notification to all those user of our app who are in certain vicinity when needed. 
 
Targeted audience:
 Nepal is a natural calamity porne country, so our  audience are are all those people who have a smart phone and are concerned about their own and their loved ones safety. 
 
Main Benefits:
1. Know your loved ones location and help them during the time of any natural calamities.
2. Search for blood, if needed.
3. an online platform to inform everyone about any volunteering program organised and join those too to help.
 
Platform: Android, iOS
