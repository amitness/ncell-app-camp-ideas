Event2Change

Submitted on:  September 6, 2015 - 12:12pm 
 
Idea:
 App to know each and every event that are happening around Nepal. Like Concerts, Exhibitions,Festivals,Jatras-Community festivles,etc.
 
Problem Solved:
Tired or missing concerts,festivels,exhibitions?
-It will provide you every details of all the events.
-No more expenses to advertise in social media, newspaper, get it all here in one single app.
 
 
Functions:
 -The app can show you the lists of Events.
-The app will notify you about the Events that you have choosen.
-The app can help buy your ticket online.
-The app will let you create your own Event.
-The app will provide group chat functionality of particular event.
 
 
Targeted audience:
 Probably everyone, who like going to events.
 
Main Benefits:
1.Easy to locate events .
2.Can buy ticket online if there is ticketing system.
3.Get notified and never miss any event you like.
4. No wasting of time surfing on the websites for the events.
 
Platform: Android
