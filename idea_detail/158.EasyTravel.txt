EasyTravel

Submitted on:  September 7, 2015 - 10:15pm 
 
Idea:
 App to track the current vehicle position and estimate the arrival time to the selected destination
 
Problem Solved:
Finding the nearest vehicle in the track and estimating its arrival time to the selected destination
 
Functions:
 It tracks all the vehicles of a route . And it finds the nearest vehicle to the selected bus stop and estimates its arrival time.
 
Targeted audience:
 All those people who use public transportation
 
Main Benefits:
1.User doesn't have to wait public vehicles longer in a boring manner
2. User can predetermine when the nearest vehicle will arrive to his bus stop
 
Platform: Android
