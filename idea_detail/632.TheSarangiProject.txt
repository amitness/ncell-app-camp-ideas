TheSarangiProject

Submitted on:  September 19, 2015 - 4:02pm 
 
Idea:
 App specialised to learn Nepalese folk music and instrument and also deals with its sales and promotion.
 
Problem Solved:
Influence of western music and modern instruments has led Nepalese folk music scenario into phase of declination. So our app will help to sculpt a land of new hope to reignite our least prevalent folk music at a global stage.
 
Functions:
 The app provides detailed informations, video tutorials ,online lessons as well as expert guiadance about Nepalese folk music in a user freindly interface to provide best user experience.
The app enables user to buy and sell their folk music and instruments also linking them in musical community.
 
Targeted audience:
 Linking passionate music lovers having keen interest in nepali folk music throughout globe who can interact and get an insight to its use and history.
 
Main Benefits:
1.It is 100% folk music oriented.
2.Easy to find tutorials about folk music.
3.Easy to buy or sell folk musical instruments.
4.Linking users to communtiy of people sharing same passion about folk music.
