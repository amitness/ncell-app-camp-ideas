PattuUdyo

Submitted on:  September 19, 2015 - 9:45pm 
 
Idea:
 A game where players put their fingers at a spot and tap on it whenever a flying thing is pronounced like pattu udyo.
 
Problem Solved:
Multiple players can play this game as a reminder of their childhood memories on their smartphone.
 
Functions:
 A touch screen where player can tap with their finger while playing is required. 
One can play the game solely for levelup (top scores) or in a network via internet or any hotspot network to be the best.
 
 
Targeted audience:
 Players who like mini games with no vast strategy requirement, but only sincere attention.
 
Main Benefits:
Multiple players can paly at once on a same screen or in a network.
 
Platform: Android
