DrivingLicenseGuru

Submitted on:  August 29, 2015 - 12:49am 
 
Idea:
 This app provides infos about training center,Documents,Rules,Q&A for getting Driving License.
 
Problem Solved:
1. Problem of being confused about  required documents.
2. Finding Training centers all over Nepal
3. Probable questions asked in written+ visual exam
4. When and where does the intake season held.
 
 
Functions:
 1. It helps to get infos about:
      -Training centers
      -Intake seasons
     -Probable questions
      -Rules and regulations
       -Documents required
 
 
Targeted audience:
 To the people who wants to get Driving License of any kind of vehicles.
 
Main Benefits:
1. Helps to understand the basic procedure for getting License.
2. Familarization towards frequently asked questions.
3. Give information about training centers and intake seasons.
 
 
Platform: Android
