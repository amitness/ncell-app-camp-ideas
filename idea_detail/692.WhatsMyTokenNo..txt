WhatsMyTokenNo.

Submitted on:  September 9, 2015 - 8:44am 
 
Idea:
 App that takes the token for you and reserve the seat.
 
Problem Solved:
This app will remove the waiting of people for their turn and directly go to related place when their turn comes. Anyone can take their token from this app.
 
Functions:
 The app registers a token number and shows what token number is being processed and informs accordingly the time left.
 
Targeted audience:
 People who needs to wait for their token number to arrive while doing any transaction.
 
Main Benefits:
1. Anyone can have their token sitting in home rather than going and waiting in queue.
 
Platform: Android, Windows phone
