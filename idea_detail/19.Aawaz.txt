Aawaz

Submitted on:  August 21, 2015 - 12:17pm 
 
Idea:
 aawaz is a social voting app that lets user ask a question and lets friends,followers,public rate the answers by choosing from the given options.
 
Problem Solved:
1. app helps users to take decisions when they are in doubt or in confusion.
2. app also takes public trending polls on politics,sport,health where idea can be generalized of people.
 
Functions:
 1. users can choose time period for which the poll will be live.
2. users can also upload picture by using camera and record sound by using mic.
3.for privacy users can make their polls visible to selected users like friends.
4.their will be also anonymous voting where faster voting can be done where username will be hidden.
5.there will be a option where users can also write feedback later. 
 
Targeted audience:
 Everyone can use this app who what to share their idea and solve problem of other rating the question.
 
 
Main Benefits:
1.  easier to make decision analyzing the poll result.
2. easier to know what people think on some hot topics.
 
Platform: Android
