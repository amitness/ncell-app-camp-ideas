HPKIDEA,VEHICLETRACK

Submitted on:  September 6, 2015 - 9:03am 
 
Idea:
 It tracks the vehicle's current position and estimates the arrival time to the selected destination.
 
Problem Solved:
Spending a lot of time to wait for public vehicles daily.
 
Functions:
 1.It locates the present location of user, nearby vehicles and the informs the user about the arrival time of the nearby vehicle to the user selected destination .
2. Infroms user about the traffic jam suffered or smooth running condition of vehicle
 
Targeted audience:
 People who use public vehicles daily
 
Main Benefits:
It saves the precious time of the people that they spend for waiting public vehicles daily. Meanwhile, they can perform others tasks.
 
Platform: Android
