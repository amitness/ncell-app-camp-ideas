PsyclotronLet'sKite

Submitted on:  September 11, 2015 - 1:30pm 
 
Idea:
 MMO strategy game for mobile platform based on realtime kite-flying promoting kite flying culture.
 
Problem Solved:
Besides entertainment, it promotes the slowly fading kite flying culture by reanimating the interest in kite flying.
 
Functions:
 -  Photorealistic environment with realtime physics
-  Head to Head and team matches
-  Skill based level ups
-  Upgradable game elements (kites, threads)
-  Global rankings (weekly, monthly and overall)
-  Shareable on social networking sites (facebook)
 
Targeted audience:
 Global audience, especially casual game lovers
 
Main Benefits:
-  Absolute entertainment
-  Realtime 3D experience
-  Online Multiplayer interaction
-  Cross platform mobile gaming
-  Global community
 
Platform: Android, iOS, Windows phone
