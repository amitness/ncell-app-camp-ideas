Idea:
 Social recommendation app.
 
Problem Solved:
People are often in confusion while buying or trying something they know less about. They are not sure which one is better and why. Examples:
If this guitar is better for me as a beginner or too expensive?
Which outfit looks nice on me this or that.
Should i travel mustang or rara lake?
This app tries to solve problem by taking reviews, recommendations or votes from people they choose. Like friends, community, hobbiest groups etc.
 
Functions:
 This app allows user to:
1) See the recommendation queries made by people.
2) Vote or comment the queries.
3) Follow people or tags they are interested in
4) Post their queries and ask for recommendation

Targeted audience:
 Young people who want to try something new, something different and have fun in life.
 
Main Benefits:
People can get honest and quick opinions on something that helps make their descision.
