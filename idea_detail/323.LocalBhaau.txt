Idea:
 An app which shares the price of local products like vegetables,fruits etc.
 
Problem Solved:
It helps to give the cheapest price of the product. A customer can share the price of the product he just bought.( products are fruits, vegetables, grains) etc.
 
Functions:
 Details of the product prices
Grocery planner.
 
Targeted audience:
 Customers, Local people.
 
Main Benefits:
The user will get to know the price.
No conflict in price.
 
Platform: Android
