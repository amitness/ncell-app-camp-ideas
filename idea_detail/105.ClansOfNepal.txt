Idea:
 Payrent
 
Problem Solved:
This application will solve the problem of people who live in rent and who give their house on rent. It will solve problem by letting them pay their rent online. And house owner don't have to ask for rent.
 
Functions:
 To pay rents of people living on rents
 
Targeted audience:
 People who give their house and flats on rent and the peoples who lives on rent
 
Main Benefits:
The user won't have to go banks to take money and pay their rents. So time will be saved. And people don't have to bother about making changes.
 
Platform: Android
