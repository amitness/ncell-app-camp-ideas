NepalExpress

Submitted on:  September 20, 2015 - 9:22am 
 
Idea:
 App to find the information about the nearby transportation routes and vehicles and cost for the service.
 
Problem Solved:
Searching the nearby transportation route of the city as well as highway,
finds the nearby local as well as a long route transportation facility nearby you if you don't know about the place you are in, for tourist who don't know about the transportation routes to roam around in city.
 
Functions:
 This app will search for all the nearby transportation facilities and show the direction to the bus stops or transportation facilities.
This app will show all the information regarding the facility and cost for using it.
This app will list down the avaliable long route transportation facilities.
 
Targeted audience:
 Tourist who don't know about the transportation route and system in Nepal,
to everyone who don't know about the transportation routes, for travellers exploring new places but don't know the routes for travel.
 
Main Benefits:
1. Easy to find the local transportation route.
2. New person in town won't get lost searching for transportation.
3. Peoples won't get forged in cost of using service.
4. Can schedule own time as per the transportation time and location.
 
Platform: Android, iOS
