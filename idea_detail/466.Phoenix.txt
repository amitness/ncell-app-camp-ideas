Idea:
 App to create live music by connectin android devices in a network where each user can select an instument and play in a group.

Individuals and music enthusiast often donot get to discover and explore their creativity in their group. Our app solves the problem and creates link between music band and an individual.

 App connect music players to a network to create live music.
 
Targeted audience:
 Individuals and music enthusiast exploring their creativity.
 
Main Benefits:
Easy and instant access to create live music from a group of individuals.
 
Platform: Android
