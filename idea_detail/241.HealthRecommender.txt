HealthRecommender

Submitted on:  September 13, 2015 - 9:02pm 
 
Idea:
 probable disease recomender,based on information provided by the user with probable disease related doctor's and hospital's information
 
Problem Solved:
1.To provide a busy scheduled person ,means to know about their health condition from anywhere
2.To provide health related service(information)
3.To help find a proper hospital and a proper doctor
 
Functions:
 1. User will have to give their general information such as: fullname,age and gender.
2. then user will have to provide symptoms they might be having and pain they are feeling 
3. our app will receive does data and proceed then give result i.e probable disease that they might be suffering from (though accuracy is limited)
4. Also when probable disease are listed, doctor's and hospital's information related to the disease are provided.
 
Targeted audience:
 All Nepalese people with age 10 and higher.
 
Main Benefits:
1. User will have knowledge about probable disease user may have or suffering from.
2. User will have proper information about doctors in nepal according to the disease.
3. Busy scheduled personel may save their time using our app.
 
 
Platform: Android
