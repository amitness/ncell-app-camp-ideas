RemoteInputDroid

Submitted on:  September 18, 2015 - 4:53pm 
 
Idea:
 A simple android application that acts as a remote for Powerpoint presentaions, Mouse & Keyboard, VLC Remote and a simple joypad.
 
Problem Solved:
This application solves problem of having to constantly be around the laptop/PC when giving a presentation, showing videos and also gives the option for multiplayer gaming.
 
Functions:
 1. Server-Client based architecture.
2. Multiple device access
3. Connection over WiFi
4. Cross platform server (Java based)
Â 
 
Targeted audience:
 The most sensible target audience for the application would be students, teachers and anyone who gives presentations on a regular basis or would like to remotely control thier PC.
 
Main Benefits:
1. Ability to control powerpoint presentations.
2. Use the mouse and keyboard remotely.
3. Control VLC Player
4. Use a joypad for gaming.
5. Localised nepali typing on computer through mobile device
 
Platform: Android
