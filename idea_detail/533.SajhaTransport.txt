SajhaTransport

Submitted on:  September 15, 2015 - 2:12pm 
 
Idea:
 Get taxi service and sajha bus arrival time information at your finger touch wherever you are.
 
Problem Solved:
1. It gives realtime location information about taxi and sajha bus which save time to get it.
2.This app help in easy recover of lost property as it display full driver information.
 
 
Functions:
 1. Get a taxi through sms as primary process as well as call.
2.Booking and Reservation of taxi,bus,micro for Domestic tour.
3. Display real time location of sajha bus with specific route with estimated arrival time.
4.Get driver detailed information for future purpose.
5.Nearby location of taxi for fast  service.
 
 
 
Targeted audience:
 Domestic tourist and International tourist
 
Main Benefits:
1. Time saving: No need to wait in order to get taxi service and sajha bus service.
2. Easy Service: Real time location of taxi,sajha bus in map makes easy to use.
3.Reservation and Booking: To make traveling efficient and easy without hassle for information and reservation.
4. Full information of taxi driver and customer.
5. Easy to recover lost property.
 
 
Platform: Android
