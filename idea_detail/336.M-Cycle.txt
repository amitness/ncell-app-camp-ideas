M-Cycle

Submitted on:  September 20, 2015 - 10:15am 
 
Idea:
 App about Menstrual Cycle.
 
Problem Solved:
It helps to keep track on the menstrual cycle that females goes through once they enter into the puberty.
 
Functions:
 Keep record of the menstrual cycle.
When and where it has been irregular.
Any serious health issues.
 
Targeted audience:
 All aged females who have been menstruating.
 
Main Benefits:
It will help monitor the menstrual cycle, its nature and irregularities.
 
Platform: Android
