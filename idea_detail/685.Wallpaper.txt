Wallpaper

Submitted on:  September 13, 2015 - 10:54pm 
 
Idea:
 Photos relating to Nepal in Wallpaper as a promotion to Nepalese Tourism
 
Problem Solved:
This app promotes the advertisement of Nepali Tourism.
 
Functions:
 The app contains the photos of natural scenes and different cultural photos related to Nepal with short introduction to those places. The photos can be used as the wallpaper of the phone.
 
Targeted audience:
 Anyone with a wish for beautiful wallpapers
 
Main Benefits:
The user can choose wide varities of wallpaper from the scenaries of nature to cultural photos as their wallpaper.
 
Platform: Android, iOS, Windows phone
