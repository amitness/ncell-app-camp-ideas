shresthaNShows

Submitted on:  September 20, 2015 - 1:29am 
 
Idea:
 Allows user to view all favourite shows schedules in one single UI broadcasting from different media channels of Nepal.
 
Problem Solved:
1)set the reminder of your favourite show.
2)notify you based on your scheduled tv shows before.
 
 
Functions:
 1) set the list of favourite shows
2)time based automated pop up notification
3)schedule recurring shows
4) Remainder of dedicated shows
5)notification based upon shows
5)Snozzing ability
 
Targeted audience:
 nepalese people from teenage to elders waiting for next shows
 
Main Benefits:
with our app, you don't need to worry that you'll forget your weekly shows or relevant.
 
Platform: Android
