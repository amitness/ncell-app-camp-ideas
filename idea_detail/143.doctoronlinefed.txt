Idea:
 Apps to trace the doctor where he is and hospital,clinic,pharmacy,nursing home,specialist,ambulance service with phone number.
 
Problem Solved:
My application would solve the problem to find doctor in an emergency case .
It would give the details of specialist doctor or physican near their locality.It also find the hospital,clinic , nursing home ,pharmacy shop,ambulance service near their locality. 
 
Functions:
 This app has list down all the specialist doctors ,hospital,clinic , nursing home ,pharmacy shop,ambulance service nearby. 
 
Targeted audience:
 All nepali citizen who can save their patient life in short span of time in an enmergency case . 
 
Main Benefits:
easy to know the doctors,hospital,clinic , nursing home ,pharmacy shop,ambulance service who are available near their locality.
easy to know the specialist who are available in their locality .
easy to know the ambulance services near their locality.
