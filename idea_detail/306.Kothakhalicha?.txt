Kothakhalicha?

Submitted on:  September 17, 2015 - 8:43am 
 
Idea:
 Kotha khalicha would be useful for all individual, business person and others around the people in nepal who are searching room, flat, house on rent. 
 
Problem Solved:
It will help to eradicate the problem of searching and finding of flat, room, house for the business person as well as individual and students to find appropriate room for living as well as room or flat to run office and small organization. Room khali6 would be the most helpful application in context of nepal.
 
Functions:
 Locate, find and easy upload of information related to flat/ room.
 
Targeted audience:
 Business person, student, family and many others who are in need.
 
Main Benefits:
Easy to locate and find room, easy for the house owner to provide notice through online app providing note about the availability of flat/room, easier for the persons to get flat/room in new area.
 
Platform: Android, iOS
