SkinConsult

Submitted on:  August 20, 2015 - 11:22pm 
 
Idea:
 Diagnose and treat skin diseases through pictures posted in the app with few information including parient particulars, symptoms.
 
Problem Solved:
Diagnose and treat skin diseases without visiting hospital.
 
Functions:
 Picture of a skin problem, mention symptoms, site of body involved, patient age, gender.
 
Targeted audience:
 Health care professionals (mbbs, health assistant, nurses) working in remote areas as well as general public.
 
Main Benefits:
Helps in diagnosis and treatment of skin diseases specially for patients and even health care providers in remote areas.
 
Platform: Android, iOS, Windows phone
