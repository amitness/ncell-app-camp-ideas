ClickOnDriver

Submitted on:  August 24, 2015 - 5:05pm 
 
Idea:
 This app track the location of the user (not in condition to drive) and sends professional driver to drop him/her home on their own vehicle.
 
Problem Solved:
Drinking and driving causes most of road accidents. Now if they are not in condition to drive then on one click professional driver will be there to help.
 
Functions:
 Now no need to worry of getting drunk at parties. There will always be professional drivers to pick the user from any location and drop them home safely.
 
Targeted audience:
 People attending parties and getting drunk and also being unable to drive home by themselves.
