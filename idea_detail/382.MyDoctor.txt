MyDoctor

Submitted on:  August 20, 2015 - 2:12am 
 
Idea:
 This app is centralizing hospital system, with give best personal heaths solution.
 
Problem Solved:
Reduce communication gaps between doctors and patient.
Save time for searching hospitals and doctors.
Easily get services from remote area.
Help to find best hospitals and doctors.
 
Functions:
  Give information about hospitals and doctors.
 Patient can directly communicate with doctors.
 Auto suggests precaution and treatment.
Take feedback with automated alarming system.
 Priority base system depending on user feedback and doctor suggestion.
 
Targeted audience:
 This app is basically helps to those who have lack of time and not able to attend physically.
This app is helpful for remote areas people.
Mostly helpful for shy people.
 
Main Benefits:
Easily can access from anywhere.
Save time for searching hospital.
Get free information about doctors, hospitals and medicine.
 
Platform: Android
