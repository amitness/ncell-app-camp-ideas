Idea:
 Convenient rent search and support service.
 
Problem Solved:
1. Tedious and exhausting search of places to rent for customers.
2. Lack of easy advertisement for rent provider.
 
 
Functions:
 1. The app lists down the rental places as per demand.
2. The app allows rental provider to post their space in a convenient way.
3. The app pins down the location based on customers interest.
 
Targeted audience:
 1. Family, students, companies, etc who are in need of rental space.
2. People eager to provide space for rent.
 
Main Benefits:
1. Easy ways to find places for rent.
2. Large scope of advertisement of rental space by rent provider.
 
 
Platform: Android, Others(Web application)
