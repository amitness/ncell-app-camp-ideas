call4blood

Submitted on:  September 19, 2015 - 4:45pm 
 
Idea:
 Building a bridge application between the blood donors and needies.
 
Problem Solved:
It eases the process for anyone to find a fresh blood.
 
Functions:
 1. Eases the gap between the blood doonors and the needies
2. Motivate the donors to continuing the good job they are doing by providing them discount card with our collaborators
3. Inspire people to do the same
 
Targeted audience:
 Active blood donors of the society and the hospital, blood banks, ingos
 
Main Benefits:
User can get fresh blood donor at his place rather than keep searching everywhere in a panic situation.
 
Platform: Android
