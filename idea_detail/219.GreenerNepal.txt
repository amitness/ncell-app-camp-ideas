GreenerNepal

Submitted on:  August 28, 2015 - 8:18pm 
 
Idea:
 When you recharge your Prepaid account for 10 times using our app, a tree will be planted in your name.
 
Problem Solved:
This application will help to restore greenary of Nepal. It helps to create a healthier  and eco-friendly environment.
 
 
Functions:
 When a user recharges his/her account from our app, a small sum of money is deduced from his account and that amount is collected in his account. When he/she has a sufficient amount, a tree will be planted in his/her name. S/he also can change that amount into freebies.
 
 
Targeted audience:
 The target audience all all the Prepaid cellphone users who recharge their phones to use the Services provided by the service provider.
 
Main Benefits:
User recharges his/her account and side by side is doing a social service. S/he  is helping to make the environment healthier for living.  Besides this, the user can exchange the plantation scheme for other freebies.
 
Platform: Android
