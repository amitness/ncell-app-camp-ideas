StockArcade

Submitted on:  September 19, 2015 - 7:15pm 
 
Idea:
 A stock market arcade game for simple fun.
 
Problem Solved:
- Provides entertainment
- Social gaming
 
Functions:
 - Has virtual companies
- User can buy and sell shares
- Share prices will change according to situations pre-notified to the user
 
Targeted audience:
 - Any user with an android phone who wants to play.
 
Main Benefits:
- Fun to play
- Pass time
- Basic Understanding of how the stock market works
 
Platform: Android
