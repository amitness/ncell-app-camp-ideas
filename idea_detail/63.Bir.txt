Idea:
 App that help you to find out the information about the relationship and bio-details of the member in the family chain or herity.
 
Problem Solved:
It's been really hard to knew the relations and relationship status in the herity family due to the lack of communication and in result, our relationship get lost with our ancestors.
So, our app can solve this problem by providing suitable platform to place family records in systematic way.
 
Functions:
 It help user to find out the information ( relationship, contact, address, location, email etc. ) about members in a family chain.
 
Targeted audience:
 Anyone can use this app, as specially targeted for family members.
 
Main Benefits:
1. Herity can be found, as all the family members can live in a good relationship by being in touch with everyone in a family chain.
2. Members can get bio-details of any members in his/her herity family.
3. It play an important role for reminding an important date like birthrate, anniversary with in family chain.
4. Relation status can be pointed out based on any member in a family chain.
 
Platform: Android
