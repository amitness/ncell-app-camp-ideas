yog-guru

Submitted on:  September 3, 2015 - 6:27pm 
 
Idea:
 "yog-guru" is an app that teaches the basics of yoga to the users.
 
Problem Solved:
Due to the decrease in physical activities and the lack of time for exeercise people have been suffering from various health related problem. When a person makes up his/her mind to be involved in some physical exercise he/she has many physical constraints to consider upon and thus procastinates his/her idea to improve fitness.
 
Functions:
 The main function include an illustrative description on how yoga is perfomed correctly.
 
Targeted audience:
 Target audience include all thepeople who want to remain fit but cannot affort sufficient time to  join a fitness club or the people who wish to remain fit but are too reluctant to go out for a walk/jogging.
 
Main Benefits:
The main benefits for the users are listed below:
1. Knowledge about yoga
2. Methods of doing yoga and meditation.
3. A list of all the places which provide training/instruction for yoga. 
 
Platform: Android
