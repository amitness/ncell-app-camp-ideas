Idea:
 In our app, you can get the location of running buses, their approx. distance and duration of arrival so that you can get ready to get the bus.
 
Problem Solved:
Where's the bus running? Is it running on schedule? Did I miss my bus? If I could have had my lunch instead of waiting here for 30 min? Now get the answers of these usual questions with our app.
 
Functions:
 The app will show the locations of buses based on the location of user and will provide approximated time and distance to reach to his/her stop.

Targeted audience:
 Middle-class people whose daily life is based on bus transport.
Students and other officer who are always worried whether they had missed their college/office bus or not.
 
Main Benefits:
Passenger can save their valueable time which they use to spend waiting at busstop.
They can get the location of their daily buses and know whether they have missed the bus or not.
Bus committee can regulate their bus service efficiently.
 
Platform: Android
