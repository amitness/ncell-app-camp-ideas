Idea:
 An app lock application that requires you to play short quiz to unlock the app.
 
Problem Solved:
Our app will especially help parents to lock apps that their children often use and will force children to play the quiz before being able to use the app.
 
Functions:
 Our applciation main functions are:
1) Lock the desire application.
2) Setup up 3 - 5 quiz randomly based on category or as desired by parents.
3) Correctly answering all questions will unlock the application
4) Quiz can be of a) choosing correct word
                            b) Matching the given word with the picture
                            c) Drawing a pattern based on question,etc
 
Targeted audience:
 Our target audience is Parents who wants to restrict access to his/her mobile from their children without correctly answering the quiz.
 
Main Benefits:
The main benefits of the app is that parents can force children to learn something new while opening the app.
 
Platform: Android
