PeoplesVoice

Submitted on:  September 20, 2015 - 11:09am 
 
Idea:
 Providing peoples views/opinions/feedback about the current scenario of the country and also  peoples problem.
 
Problem Solved:
The government can use peoples idea in different sectors if they are useful and also  consider peoples problem and try to solve it. For eg: the constitution of our country is in its peak and we can give our opinion regarding it.
 
Functions:
 The app will show the different sectors in which users want to provide their opinion/feedback .They can also include their society's problem. They can also look after others problem and feedback.
 
Targeted audience:
 teenager/man/woman
 
Main Benefits:
They can give feedback, share their opinions and also solve their own/society's problem.  It helps to know peoples ideas regarding different sectors like education,politics, social problems etc.
 
Platform: Android
