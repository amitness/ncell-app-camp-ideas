HospiTele

Submitted on:  September 20, 2015 - 10:41am 
 
Idea:
 Your home/app based medical solution.
 
Problem Solved:
1.Patients have to wait for hours in queue even for the usual/normal medical consultation, So with the help of this app they don't have to wait for hours and can access doctor from home.
2.Medical Consultation can be accessed anywhere in Nepal.
3.Solution for routine illness.
4.Tourists seeking medical consultation in Nepal.
 
Functions:
 1.Video Conference with doctor.
2.Can set appointments.
3.Appointments remainder.
4.Electronic Prescription.
5.Hospital/Pharmacies on map.
 
 
Targeted audience:
 Students,Old age homes,Orphanages,Hotels and tourists and General peoples.
 
Main Benefits:
1. Can book appointments with comfort of home.
2. Less time consuming.
3. Easy doctor access and 24/7 service.
4. Electronic prescription.
5. First free appointment
6. Easy payment systems.
7. Easy remainder and less expensive with respect to consultation.
 
 
Platform: Android
