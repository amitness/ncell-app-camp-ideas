Idea:
 An edutainment game that familiarize with the basics of chemical reaction/composition by letting them play the game which enhances their concept.
 
Problem Solved:
 A subject mater taught side by side with entertainment is most efficient.
People have hard time dealing with chemistry during different phases of their life. As a part of basic home science it is important to understand those concepts. We therefore present those concepts in a entertaining game that enhances their concepts related to chemistry
 
Functions:
 - lets users to combine different chemicals.
- Share score in social media.
- In game purchases.
 
Targeted audience:
 - Students/ Teacher (higher secondary students)(primary target)
 
- Parents (who help on doing their children's homework)
 
Main Benefits:
- it also provides its user their own portable laboratory
- this will increase the grades of the students
- edutainment learning
- helps to over exam phobia
- effective and effecient learning
- creativity
 
Platform: Android, iOS, Windows phone, Others(Web app)
