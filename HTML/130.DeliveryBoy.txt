DeliveryBoy

Submitted on:  September 19, 2015 - 7:53pm 
 
Idea:
 Reliable and instant delivery of product from online shopkeepers(facebook fanpage/e-commerce) with constant online checking or update of product route
 
Problem Solved:
The main problem solved by our application are:
1.Online Buyers are convinced about their purchased product current position from online with time and location description.
2.Online Seller are reliable about their product delivery process until their buyers gets product.
3.Online seller/buyer needn't need to call constantly about location and time.
4.People mainly from outside valley can easily shop online with reliability of products route and time.
 
Functions:
 The main functions and elements of our application are:
1.Upto date GPS location of product delivery person.
2.Full description of products starting and ending location.
 
Targeted audience:
 Our target audience are:
1.E-commerce websites or facebook fanpages who sell product online.
2.Online buyers who always want to be convinced about their purchased product's location and time.
 
 
 
Main Benefits:
There are three types of users who can take advantage from our application.
They are:
1.Online Shopkeepers(Facebook fanpage/E-commerce): They can deliver product instantly with constant update of product position or route with GPS location tracking, as they do not need to call about buyers location repeatedly.
2.Buyers: They can get location and time update about their purchased product route .
3. Anyone can create account in our application and deliver product and earn money.
 
 
Platform: Android
