NepaliAwaaz

Submitted on:  September 8, 2015 - 5:02pm 
 
Idea:
 Our App - NepaliAwaaz helps the voice of Nepalese to be heard by the Government
 
Problem Solved:
Strikes, Curfews have been a major issue of Nepalese Economy. In such a scenarion, Our App solves by making our rights more prominent and our demands more feasible
 
Functions:
 This app has a text and voice based messaging service to talk to the concerned authorities and also consists of social media integration - Facebook, Twitter, Viber, WhatsApp
 
Targeted audience:
 Nepalese people globally
 
Main Benefits:
To make it easier to talk to the Government without the use of violence.
 
Platform: Android
