PATH(asingletouch)

Submitted on:  August 27, 2015 - 12:53pm 
 
Idea:
 PATH a single touch app(public and the hospital) which would help to show right path to public in field of health sector through a single touch.
 
Problem Solved:
It will help general public in emergency at any where and in any situation to find hospitals,doctors of particular field at particular time.
 
Functions:
 -Easy to search nearest hospitals and doctors at particular time.
-Alarming system which will help both doctors and patient to coordinate with each other.
-Fast treatment.
-Adds value to heath sector.
-Saves life through a single touch.
 
Targeted audience:
 The target audience for app are all general public specially for the people who need emergency services.
 
Main Benefits:
- GPS location based nearest hospitals search(area)
- One click registration through google account.
-Show doctors  avaliable at particular time
-Displays calculated nearest hospitals on map with approximate distance.
 
 
Platform: Android
