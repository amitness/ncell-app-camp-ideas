Cyclepanti

Submitted on:  September 18, 2015 - 8:52am 
 
Idea:
 A game that simulates cycling through iconic alleyways, monuments and natural beauty of Nepal.
 
Problem Solved:
Currently, there aren't many games that reflect Nepali uniqueness. This will help promote Nepal's unique sights and culture.
 
Functions:
 A cycling simulator that takes you through iconic locations in Nepal. It will have achievements for best time, longest route, etc.
 
Targeted audience:
 Teenagers. Kids. Adults. Everyone.
 
Main Benefits:
FUN.
 
Platform: Android, iOS, Windows phone
