SurviveKtmCity

Submitted on:  September 20, 2015 - 7:37am 
 
Idea:
 "Survive Ktm City" is a unique single player mobile game application where player has to survive a motorbike ride in Kathmandu city.
 
Problem Solved:
This game is more of an entertainment than a problem solver. Nevertheless, it will definitely serve a good purpose in promoting Tourism industry.
 
Functions:
 1) Game map will be designed from Kathmandu's street maps which will contain popular locations and landmarks of Kathmandu city.
2) There will be different levels in the game like (a) surviving against the ignorant drivers of the city, (b) surviving a ride in Nepal bandha/Andolan, (c) surviving during Earthquake, (d) surviving in polluted areas etc.
 
 
 
Targeted audience:
 Most of the audience should be Nepalese as they will be more familiar with the streets of Kathmandu. However, users from around the world are expected.
 
Main Benefits:
1) Fun and entertainment.
2) Get familiar with streets and popular landmarks of Kathmandu.
3) Get educated about about some of the prevailing problems that exist in streets of Kathmandu valley.
 
 
Platform: Android, Others(Unity, C#)
