Idea:
 App that lets you travel on a budget by suggesting the cheapest flights, accommodations and much more so that you can just Backpack and get going.
 
Problem Solved:
Travelling is not cheap. And with the overwhelming number of hotels and hangouts, when on a budget, tourists find it tough to locate the cheapest ones. Our app helps solve this problem.
 
Functions:
 The app will suggest the cheapest hotels and restaurants and other places of interest based on the budget of the user and the location he/she is in.
It will also list out the cheapest flights to their destination if they are travelling abroad.
The app notifies about any upcoming deals or discounts nearby the user’s location which might be of interest to the user. 
 
Targeted audience:
 Tourists as well as locals who want to travel cheap. Also, students who can’t afford to spend a lot on hotels and flights.
 
Main Benefits:
1. Allows tourists as well as locals to locate the cheapest hotels and restaurants.
2. Notifies them about special deals and/or discounts in places of interest.
 
Platform: Android
