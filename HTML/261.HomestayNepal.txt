HomestayNepal

Submitted on:  September 3, 2015 - 3:31pm 
 
Idea:
 Helps connect local/foreign tourists with local residency.
 
Problem Solved:
Unsettled rooms can be rented for one or more night/day stay.
local people can be employeed.
 
 
Functions:
 Tourists would be able to rent a stay.
Homestays can advertise their rooms.
 
Targeted audience:
 Local / Foreign Tourists visiting Nepal.
 
Main Benefits:
Tourists can search for local homestay without going to town to town.
Tourists can book a rent.
Homestay's can avail their unused rooms.
Local people can earn money.
 
 
Platform: Android, Others(Web)
