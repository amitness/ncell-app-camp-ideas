Idea:
 This is about a quest of a young warrior to reunit with his lover, who is kidnapped by an Evil Wizard.
 
Problem Solved:
This will provide funtime to users.
Makes spare time enjoyable.
 
Functions:
 Generic 2d platform game.
Based on reward system.
Player will pass through different levels to complete his quest.
As the level increases the player will get power ups.
Enemies also will become more stronger as the level  increases.
 
 
Targeted audience:
 Youngsters.
Those who love playing oldschool platform game.
 
Main Benefits:
User will be able to kill thier boredom.
Improve problem solving skills of user by interacting with diferent game elements.
 
Platform: Android
