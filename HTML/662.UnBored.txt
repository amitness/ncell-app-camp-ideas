UnBored

Submitted on:  September 14, 2015 - 9:58pm 
 
Idea:
 "UnBored" is an AI app, whose single tap suggests you the best possible thing you can do, knows your context too and learns itself.
 
Problem Solved:
"UnBored" is a personal assitant which learns by itself, knows your context, help to find stuff to do when you are bored.
Scene 1: you are travelling on a train and you are bored
 suggestion: Read article on Fastest trains around the world
Scene 2: You are at home, it's 8 pm and you are bored:
 suggestion: HBO is showing "Transformer" watch it.
and It continues to learn.
 
 
 
Functions:
 The main functions/element of the app is to provide the recommendation engine curated for the individual's best interest.
 
Targeted audience:
 Anyone who is eligible to carry a smartphone
 
Main Benefits:
"UnBored" is a well integrated app, which uses all the services provided by Google search, Google Places, IMDB, Facebook, Twitter. It customizes itself to provide the best suggestions. 
 
Platform: Android, iOS
