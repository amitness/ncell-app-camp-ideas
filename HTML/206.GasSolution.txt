GasSolution

Submitted on:  August 23, 2015 - 12:04pm 
 
Idea:
 An android app which displays the current gas cylinder rate and sends gas cylinder request to desired gas cylinder providers.
 
Problem Solved:
 1. Roaming different places in search of gas cylinder providers.
2. Fraud in the name of availability of gas cylinders.
3. No business in strikes specially called as 'Banda'. 
 
 
 
Functions:
 1. To display the present gas cylinder rate all over Nepal.
2. To search all the nearby gas cylinder providers within the range of 5 km distance.
3. To directly communicate with the gas cylinder providers. 
 
Targeted audience:
 People all over the country who are facing the problem of gas cylinders. 
 
 
 
Main Benefits:
1. Users can search all the nearby gas cylinder providers anytime anywhere.
2. Users can receive information about the present gas cylinder rate all over nepal.
3. Users can directly communicate with the gas cylinder providers.
4. Most importantly, business is not affected during 'Banda' (s).
 
Platform: Android
