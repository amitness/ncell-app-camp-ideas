Data-Lab-Nepal

Submitted on:  August 21, 2015 - 1:36pm 
 
Idea:
 Citing the lack of digital data regarding Nepalese business, politics, film industries etc., we want to build a data lab inspiring people to use
 
Problem Solved:
Availability of data related to Nepal as much as we can find about other countries over the internet
 
Functions:
 Inspire people to use and contribute to the data repository.
 
Targeted audience:
 Business people all over the world, Entrepreneurs, local market
 
Main Benefits:
People all over the world and business people can find important data as easily and as much as possible.
 
Platform: Android
