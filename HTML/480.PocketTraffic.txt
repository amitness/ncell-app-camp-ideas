PocketTraffic

Submitted on:  September 1, 2015 - 7:14pm 
 
Idea:
 This application helps to get information about traffics, traffics jams and road conditions
 
Problem Solved:
It helps to prevent from getting huge traffic jams and helps to find out open shortest path to reach in distinction point on time
 
Functions:
 It update the current road conditions ,traffic pressure and jams 
 
Targeted audience:
 Especially to those who are involved in driving jobs (ambulance, schools buses and vans, official vehicles)
 
Main Benefits:
1.Easy to reach in distinction point on time
2. Prevent from getting huge traffic jams
 
Platform: Android
