Dr.Diary

Submitted on:  September 15, 2015 - 4:43pm 
 
Idea:
 This is the unique medical application that provides many features to save time for busy Doctors, specially for clinics.
 
Problem Solved:
1. Ease time management for Doctors.
2. Easy patient reports veiwing.
3. Easy to schedule appointment. 
 
 
Functions:
 1. Add and view the patient information.
2. Add and view the appointment.
3.Add and view photo reports (X-ray, ECG).
4.Add and view treatment details.
 
Targeted audience:
 All Categories Doctors.
 
Main Benefits:
1. Doctors can easily manage time.
2. Previous reports on patient can be viewed upon needed.
3. Schedule Doctor's appointment properly.
 
Platform: Android
