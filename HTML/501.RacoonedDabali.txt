Idea:
 Student Guide for their career according to their interest.
 
Problem Solved:
Student's dilemma while selecting college within their reach and interest.
 
Functions:
 Functions:
This app takes the students to a virtual tour to all the colleges as per their preferred courses and locations.
Elements:
1) Courses and their details
2) Colleges and their Details
3) College Reviews from the current students
 
 
Targeted audience:
 1) Students Searching for Colleges: Choosing college has always been a stumbling-block for students.A student graduate, whether SLC or +2 or PCL, and they are the ones who we want to assist.
 
 
Main Benefits:
1) Saves Time and Money:  Students don't have to visit each and every college personally.
2) Simplifies College Selection: This app avails the students with the details of all the courses they are open to select and the respective colleges.
3) Getting to know the Insights of College: Users can peek inside the college even before enrollment on the basis of reviews of current students .
 
 
 
Platform: Android
