Submitted on:  September 18, 2015 - 5:53pm 
 
Idea:
 Try out virtual eyewear and find out the one that fits you.See the products in 3d at home before you buy..
 
 
Problem Solved:
Allows consumers to try on and see commercial products from the app without actually stepping out of their home.
 
It enhances products previews, such as allowing customers to view the products in 3d space in real time, just from plain materials. 
 
 
Functions:
  
Utilizing the front facing camera of the smart phone and its display, the app allows you to view 3d rendered models in real world environment by scanning the targets.
  
It also allows to render virtual eyewear by using eye tracking system to translate the user's eye movement into the screen.
 
Targeted audience:
 Everyone
 
Main Benefits:
Printed products can be triggered to create interactive content to engage the users. 
 Enhances consumer experience by allowing them to try on virtual products before buying.
 
 
Platform: Android
