Idea:
 To develop an app that will help to prepare the best possible food item from the ingredients provided by the user as well as provide other food recipe
 
Problem Solved:
1. People may not have time and money to eat outside. They may also not have all the ingredients int the recipe to prepare food in their own kitchen. So it helps to find the best recipe for the available ingredients.
2. People might have problem in estimating the time required for each process in recipe. It helps to estimate the time by using timer.
