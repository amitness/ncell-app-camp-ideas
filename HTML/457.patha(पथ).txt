Idea:
 A social app for Tourists which allows to connect and communicate to tourists, people, villages, hotels nearby.
 
Problem Solved:
Tourism is adventure, far from city tourists may be in a lonely place and sometimes be in some problems like sickness, injury, food shortage, or even worse. Our project will help them with these and make the tour a lot fun.
 
Functions:
 1. Connection with the other tourists nearby,
2. Track and follow someone in the connection,
3. Star people to know where they are, follow them,
4. Broadcast help message in need of emergency,
5. Location information of nearby homes, hotels or villages and path to them,
6. Single button broadcast which automatically sends gps info and location to the nearby apps.
 
Targeted audience:
 Most useful to tourists in rural regions, the app is suitable for other people who are in the similar field.
 
Main Benefits:
1. Users will know people who are nearby although they are travelling alone, they will not be alone,
2. Users will be able to follow people who are marching to the same destination making them easy to find the path,
3. They will be able to communicate with the people nearby,
4. They will be able to find any kind of help with the help of  simple broadcast meassage facility.
 
Platform: Android, SMS
