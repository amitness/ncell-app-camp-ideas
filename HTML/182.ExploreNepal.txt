ExploreNepal

Submitted on:  September 17, 2015 - 3:21pm 
 
Idea:
 This app allows all the national and international tourist to know about the religious and cultural events happeneing in the country.
 
Problem Solved:
Our app helps the international tourists to explore Nepal's cultural and religious diversities. It also helps internal tourists to explore our country further.
 
Functions:
 This app shows all the major and minor celebrations happening in Nepal and the reasons behind their celebration.
This app also allows users to upload their events and invite their friends from phone book to their event via app.
 
Targeted audience:
 This app can be used by any person who wants to explore the cultural and religious diversity of Nepal
 
Main Benefits:
The user gets to know all the events happening in the country and they get to post their own events like concerts, parties and invite thier friends.
