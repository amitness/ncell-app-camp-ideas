Idea:
 Testing hearing capacity and surrounding noise level
 
Problem Solved:
Noise pollution has been given least concern but has more adverse effects on peoples health. Our app helps to find their hearing capacity and noise level of the daily exposed surroundings, so that user can take precautions.
 
Functions:
 - check hearing capacity of the user- notify user if the surrounding noise level is harmful
 
Targeted audience:
 everybody
 
Main Benefits:
- minimize the health effects due to noise pollution- users can know if their surrounding is noisy or not
 
Platform: Android
