FootballNepal

Submitted on:  September 20, 2015 - 11:31am 
 
Idea:
 This app provides informations and news regarding the domestic football league of Nepal.
 
Problem Solved:
Entertains the people by providing scores updates and news of football.
 
Functions:
 This will provide news, videos, photos and provides live score to the Football Fans.
 
Targeted audience:
 All football lovers and people who wants to uplift Nepali football.
 
Main Benefits:
They can still enjoy domestic league despite getting getting busy in their respective works.
 
Platform: Android
