Idea:
 Team up and enjoy multiplayer battle field with your own country fellows. Find online players form different part of your country.
 
Problem Solved:
No more unknown players who can't communicate in your team. Find game geeks nearby you or from your country. Know the most popular online multiplayer game in your area. Make new online friends similar to your type.  
 
Functions:
 1. Helps you make nearby online mate.
2. Advertise online and popular games.
3. Promote virtual games among all age people our country.
4. This app could  get viral in international market.
 
Targeted audience:
 All smart phone users plays online games. This app will be popular with school and collage students who are interested in online multiplayer games like Clash of Clans, Respawnables, Candy crush, etc. Also the players who want to increase their friend circle.
 
Main Benefits:
1.     You won’t be bore as you are aligning or challenging the players form your country.
2.     You might not know there are other COC players in your school or college with this app you can find seniors and juniors for your own school or college clan.
3.     Being a game geek your friend circle will increase.
