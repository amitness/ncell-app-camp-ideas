FestiveFood

Submitted on:  September 20, 2015 - 10:55am 
 
Idea:
 App to know the festival special food and guides you to make those food, apparatus required.
 
Problem Solved:
Solve the tension of having problem in making food in festivals and any occassion.
 
Functions:
 1)Menus that allows you to choose food and view process to make that food.
2)Festival special food and algorithm to make those food.
 
Targeted audience:
 Every Nepalalese users and to those who wants to taste nepali food.
 
Main Benefits:
User can open the app and learn about the process of making food in their festivals and any occassion.
 
Platform: Android
