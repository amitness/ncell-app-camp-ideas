Idea:
 Multiple Language(used in nepal)  converter.
 
Problem Solved:
Due to the vast language spoken in Nepal, our application will allow people to convert the words of different language spoken in Nepal to English.
 
 
Functions:
 THe main functions of our application are:
1) Translate words to English
2) Show the meaning of the word
3) Find list of languages spoken within an area.
4) Learn Language.
5) Hear the pronounciation of the given word.
 
Targeted audience:
 Our target audience are all the people travelling or within nepal and to help them easily understand each other by helping to break the language barrier.
 
Main Benefits:
The main benefits to the users is that they can easily find the meaning of the spoken language.
 
 
Platform: Android
