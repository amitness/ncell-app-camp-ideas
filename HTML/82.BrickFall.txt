Idea:
 Brick fall is a android game in which a user tries to build a towers of bricks falling from above. user needs to balance the tower from falling.
 
Problem Solved:
It is for the entertainment and gaming.
 
Functions:
 This game is targeted for all age group for playing. 
It has easy touch controls.
Top scorer are listed and their high score can be linked in social websites for sharing.
 
 
Targeted audience:
 All age group are the target audience for this app.
People who like games like angry bird, flappy bird, subway surfer will like this game.
 
Main Benefits:
The user can compete online with other users and beat the top scorer. There will be the short listing of top 5 playesrs.
 
Platform: Android
