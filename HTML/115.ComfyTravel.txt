ComfyTravel

Submitted on:  September 19, 2015 - 12:27pm 
 
Idea:
 Build a social network of willing private vehical owners and non-owners to create database of starting and ending point of pre-planned travel.
 
Problem Solved:
People are forced to travel in hard way in public vehicles when private vehicles with same destination as theirs is travelling empty.
 
Functions:
 It will be a social network of private vehicle owners and non-owners. The private vehical owners will update their starting and ending point of travel with the date and time of travel. The owners and non-owners can mail each other to come at understanding of their mutual benefits.
There will be separate database for long distance travels and day to day travels.
 
Targeted audience:
 Anyone who want comfortable ride.
 
Main Benefits:
Users avoid painful public vehicles to have a comfortable ride.
 
Platform: Android, iOS
