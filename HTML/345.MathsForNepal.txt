MathsForNepal

Submitted on:  August 21, 2015 - 10:03pm 
 
Idea:
 The Maths For Nepal app is an app for the Nepali students to learn maths and become an expert in maths to build their career.
 
Problem Solved:
The App helps the students to learn complicated mathematics in an easy way.
 
Functions:
 It will be like a real teacher, It is an app that will be able to solve mathematical problems,explain it step by step and test the maths skills of students.
 
Targeted audience:
 This app will be built for students for all level of mathematics.
 
Main Benefits:
The users can learn mathematics easily,it will make the students experts at maths and helps students to understand maths step by step
 
Platform: Android, iOS, Windows phone, Others(Symbian mobiles and even windows PCs)
