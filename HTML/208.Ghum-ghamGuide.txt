Submitted on:  September 17, 2015 - 11:06am 
 
Idea:
 App to guide for travelling historical places in  Nepal.
 
Problem Solved:
Nowadays, it has become difficult to find a guide who is well known about  most of the historical places. So,this app is to provide facility of pesonal guide for visiting different places.
 
Functions:
 1.It provide the map to know about the routes of the visiting places.
2.It is useful for knowing about the current weather condition before visiting the place.
3.The main feature of this app will be getting detail information about the place just by clicking a single picture. After clicking the picture (eg.temple,monuments etc.) using this app, detail information of the place will appear on the mobile screen.
 
Targeted audience:
 Mainly targeted to national and international tourist who love travelling.
It can be feasible for the every age of people.
 
Main Benefits:
1.Easy to know the route and recent weather of the place one wish/want to visit.
2.To get the information about where and what kind of public transportation is available.
3.Easy to get the details about the historical places ( temple,monuments,palace area etc.) by clicking the picture of the place.
 
Platform: Android
